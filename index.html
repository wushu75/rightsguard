Module 3 · Section 8 Grounds Documentation

Section 21 is gone.
Section 8 evidence built automatically.

With no-fault evictions abolished under RRA 2025, every possession must be proven through Section 8 grounds. RightsGuard tracks incidents — rent arrears, damage, antisocial behaviour — and builds the evidence file automatically over time.

🚫
Section 21 abolished from 1 May 2026. Any Section 21 notices served after this date are unlawful. All possession proceedings must now use Section 8 with documented grounds. RightsGuard tracks those grounds automatically.
